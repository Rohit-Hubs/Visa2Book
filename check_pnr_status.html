PNR Status FAQs

Common questions about PNR status checking

What is a PNR number?

PNR (Passenger Name Record) is a unique 10-digit number assigned to your booking when you make a reservation. It helps identify your journey details in the system.

Where can I find my PNR number?

Your PNR number is provided in your booking confirmation email and SMS. It's also printed on your e-ticket or physical ticket.

How often is PNR status updated?

PNR status is updated in real-time as changes occur in the system. However, for flights, the status typically becomes final 48 hours before departure.

What does "Waitlisted" status mean?

A Waitlisted status means your booking hasn't been confirmed yet. You'll be automatically confirmed if seats become available due to cancellations.

Can I check PNR status without internet?

Yes, you can check PNR status via SMS by sending your PNR number to the designated railway/airline shortcode.
