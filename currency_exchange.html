Currency Exchange FAQs

Answers to common questions about foreign exchange

How do I get the best exchange rate?

To get the best exchange rates:

  • Compare rates: Check our live rates against banks and other providers
  • Avoid airports: Airport exchange counters typically offer poor rates
  • Exchange larger amounts: We offer better rates for higher value transactions
  • Use limit orders: Set your target rate and we'll exchange when it's reached
  • Watch market trends: Exchange when your target currency is strong

Our rate alert feature can notify you when your desired rate is available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for currency exchange:

  • Bank transfer: Most secure method with best rates
  • Credit/Debit cards: Visa, Mastercard, Amex (2.5% fee applies)
  • UPI/NetBanking: For instant INR transfers
  • Cash: At our physical branches only
  • Digital wallets: PayPal, Paytm, and others

For amounts over ₹200,000, bank transfer is required due to regulatory requirements.

How long does currency delivery take?

Delivery times depend on your location and the currency:

  • Major cities: Next-day delivery for orders placed before 2pm
  • Tier 2/3 cities: 2-3 business days
  • Airport pickup: Available within 4 hours at select airports
  • Exotic currencies: May take 3-5 business days as we source them

We provide real-time tracking for all currency deliveries. For urgent needs, our airport kiosks offer 50+ currencies for immediate pickup.

Is there a limit on how much currency I can exchange?

Exchange limits vary based on regulations and payment method:

  • Online transactions: Up to ₹1,000,000 per transaction
  • In-person exchanges: Up to ₹500,000 without documentation
  • Foreign currency: No limit on buying, but RBI restricts selling INR for foreign currency
  • Larger amounts: Requires PAN card and additional KYC documentation

For amounts over ₹10,00,000, please contact our corporate team for special rates and arrangements.
