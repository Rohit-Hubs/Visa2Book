Refund Policy

Understand how refunds work for your bookings

Our Refund Policy

At Visa2Book, we strive to make the refund process as transparent and straightforward as possible. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are issued for flight, train, and hotel bookings made through our platform.

Important Note

Refund processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment method. Typically, refunds take 7-14 business days to reflect in your account after processing.

Flight Booking Refunds

Refund eligibility and amounts for flight bookings depend on the airline's policy and fare rules.

Cancellation Time Refund Type Processing Fee
More than 48 hours before departure Full refund (as per fare rules) ₹500 or 5% of ticket value (whichever is higher)
24-48 hours before departure Partial refund (airline dependent) ₹750 or 10% of ticket value
Less than 24 hours before departure No refund (except cancellations by airline) N/A
Refund Process for Flights
  1. Submit cancellation request through our platform or mobile app
  2. We process your request with the airline (1-2 business days)
  3. Airline approves/denies refund based on fare rules (1-3 business days)
  4. If approved, we process refund to your original payment method (3-5 business days)
  5. Amount reflects in your account (depends on your bank)

Train Booking Refunds

Indian Railways has specific refund rules based on when the ticket is cancelled:

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Deductions
More than 48 hours before departure 90% of ticket value Flat ₹60 + 10% of fare
12-48 hours before departure 75% of ticket value Flat ₹60 + 25% of fare
Less than 12 hours before departure 50% of ticket value Flat ₹60 + 50% of fare
After chart preparation No refund (TDR filing possible) N/A
Important Note for Tatkal Tickets

Tatkal tickets are non-refundable except in case of train cancellation or if the journey is not undertaken.

Hotel Booking Refunds

Hotel refund policies vary by property. Here are our general guidelines:

  • Free Cancellation: Full refund if cancelled within property's specified period (usually 24-72 hours before check-in)
  • Non-refundable Rates: No refund for cancellations (may allow date changes with fee)
  • Early Check-out: No refund for unused nights unless specified in property policy
  • No-show: Full booking amount may be charged

How to Request a Refund

Follow these steps to request a refund:

Online Refund Request
  1. Log in to your Visa2Book account
  2. Go to "My Trips" section
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel
  4. Click "Request Refund" and follow instructions
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email with refund details
Customer Support

If you're unable to process the refund online, contact our customer support team:

  • Email: refunds@visa2book.com
  • Phone: +91 98765 43210 (24/7)
  • Live Chat: Available on our website and mobile app

Exceptions & Special Cases

In certain situations, special refund rules may apply:

Flight Cancellations

If your flight is cancelled by the airline, you're entitled to a full refund or alternative flight as per DGCA guidelines.

Train Cancellations

For trains cancelled by Indian Railways, full refund is processed automatically for e-tickets.

Force Majeure

For natural disasters or government restrictions, special refund policies may apply.

Visa Rejection

With proper documentation, we may waive cancellation fees for visa rejections.
